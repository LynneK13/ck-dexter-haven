Owned by Jim Kernodle, City Videography has been creating award winning, PROFESSIONAL photography and videography for 30 years. With a UNIQUE CINEMATIC STYLE, we will match the style of the video with the bride's wishes. Our expertise enables us to obtain clear, crisp video footage of your wedding UNOBTRUSIVELY and inconspicuously by "blending in" with your guests. Our post-production skills and equipment then enable us to produce a TIMELESS cinematic memory of your wedding that you will cherish forever.

Recognition for Jim's work began with his first photographic award at the age of 16 from Scholastic Art Awards. Through the years, Jim has received the Wetzlar, Germany, Leica School of Photography Award of Europe, 16mm Award of Excellence US Military, photographic awards from the International School of Photography of Winona, and film awards from UPTN for Excellence in Coverage. Jim now focuses full time on capturing beautiful weddings on video.
